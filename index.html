ARUN KUMAR

Arun Kumar over the years, has carved a niche for himself in the world of percussion as one of the few drummers who does inter-disciplinary music. He has trained for jazz drumming under his father B. S. Sukumar and the renowned drummer Ranjit Barot. Arun also trained in Carnatic percussions under Sri S. V. Giridhar and Sri Anoor Anantha Krishna Sharma. He was initiated to Carnatic music from the Legendary Vocalist 'Karnataka kala tilaka' Vidwan Anoor Suryanarayana.

For the past 20 years, Arun has been sharing the stage with Indian classical music’s geniuses like Sangita Kalanidhi Dr. R. K. Srikantan, Sangita Kalanidhi Dr. M. Balamuralikrishna, Sangita Kalanidhi A. K. C. Natarajan, Ghatam maestro Vikku Vinayakram, Tabla maestro Ustad Zakir Hussain, Sangita Kalanidhi Trichy Sankaran, Dr. L. Subramaniam, Ganakalshree M. S. Sheela, Sangita Kalanidhi Sudha Raghunathan, Nithyashree Mahadevan, Vijay Siva, Abhishek Raghuram, Pattabiram Pandit, Dr. Kadri Gopalnath, Gottuvadyam N. Ravikiran, Ganesh Kumaresh, Nagraj & Dr. Manjunath, Veenai Dr. Jayanthi Kumaresh, Dr Suma Sudhindra, Lalgudi G. J. R. Krishnan & Lalgudi VijayaLakshmi, Ronu Muzumdar, Rakesh Chaurasia, Mandolin Wizard U. Rajesh, Tabla maestro Bickram Ghosh, Pandit Vijay Ghate, Khanjira maestro Selva Ganesh, Veena maestro Rajesh Vaidya & Stephan Devassy.
